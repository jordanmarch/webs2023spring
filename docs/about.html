About Me

When I'm not busy with academics, I spend a lot of my time on personal creative projects. That might mean I'm painting a canvas, jotting down a poem, attempting to write a screenplay, or running around somewhere with my camera. If I'm not in Pittsburgh, I'm probably visiting home or exploring somewhere new. I love to get outdoors whether that be hiking, backpacking, or laying on the beach. I'm trying to tick all the national parks off my list, but still have a ways to go.

I'm currently a junior at the University of Pittsburgh, double majoring in Communication Rhetoric and Media & Professional Communications, and pursuing a certificate in Digital Media. That's a mouthful. I love to read, write, research, and design.

In my time at Pitt, I've held a heavy focus on analyzing rhetoric and media through an intersectional feminist lens. I'm currently conducting research on the evolution of university sexual harassment policy and how rhetoric functions to communicate cultural stances on sexual harassment and gender equity in our ever-changing world.

I also work as a creative intern at Ethic Advertising Agency. In this role I spend a lot of my time designing print and digital advertisements for clients, developing ideas and scripts for client audio and video advertisments, and designing in-house on brand creative for the agency. I absolutely love it and am always learning new and valuable things about the industry. One day I would love to work as a creative director.
