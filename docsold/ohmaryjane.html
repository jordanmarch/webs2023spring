Mary Jane!

Oh, Mary Jane!

PDF Link

This is a print design for a poster advertising mary jane shoes from the No. 6 Store. I wanted to play around with non-angular elements, something I find challenging. I chose to do this for mary jane shoes which I find playful and think are coming back (or never went out of style), despite what others may say.
